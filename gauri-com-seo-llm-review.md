## Executive Summary

Gauri.com is a well-structured WordPress site with good content hierarchy, strong client testimonials, and a solid Salesforce AppExchange presence (124 reviews, 5-star rating). However, several critical issues undermine its SEO effectiveness and LLM discoverability:

- **Broken sitemap** (500 error) prevents efficient search engine crawling
- **No presence on Clutch.co, G2, or GoodFirms** -- the platforms LLMs rely on most for IT company recommendations
- **Auto-generated `llms.txt`** is too thin to give LLMs meaningful context; no `llms-full.txt` exists
- **Severely stale content** -- only 1 resource published in the last 2 years (Feb 2026)
- **Privacy Policy references the wrong act ("1988" instead of "1998") and predates GDPR** -- potential compliance risk
- **Multiple duplicate URLs** for the same services causing potential canonicalization issues
- **Missing Schema.org structured data** across all pages

**Overall Verdict:** The site has strong foundational content and real client proof points, but is significantly underperforming in technical SEO, content freshness, and LLM-era discoverability. When a prospect asks an LLM to recommend a Salesforce/SAP consulting partner in the UK, Gauri is unlikely to be suggested despite being a legitimate Salesforce Summit Partner.
